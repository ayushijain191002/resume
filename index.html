Gupshup Chat Application

Gupshup - Real-Time Chat Application

Designed a comprehensive real-time communication platform that revolutionizes how users connect and interact. Gupshup features an intuitive messaging interface with seamless real-time synchronization, ensuring instant message delivery and read receipts for enhanced communication flow.

The application includes high-quality video and voice calling capabilities with crystal-clear audio-visual experience, screen sharing functionality, and group calling support for up to 50 participants. The design prioritizes user experience with features like typing indicators, message reactions, file sharing up to 100MB, and rich media support including images, videos, and documents.

Key design highlights include a clean, modern interface with customizable themes (light/dark mode), smart contact management with status updates, end-to-end encryption indicators for privacy-conscious users, and innovative features like disappearing messages and chat backup. The UI showcases smooth transitions, micro-interactions, and a carefully crafted information architecture that makes navigation effortless even during complex multi-tasking scenarios.

Reelio Short Video Platform

Reelio - Short Video Social Platform

Designed India's next-generation short video platform that empowers creators to share their stories through engaging vertical video content. Reelio combines the best of social media interaction with seamless content creation tools, offering users an intuitive interface for recording, editing, and sharing 15-60 second videos that capture moments, talents, and creativity.

The platform features a powerful in-app video editor with multi-layer editing capabilities, 100+ filters and effects, trending audio library with millions of songs, AR face filters, beauty modes, and speed controls. Users can easily add text overlays, stickers, transitions, and green screen effects. The content discovery algorithm is designed to surface relevant content through personalized For You feeds, trending hashtags, and challenge-based content organization.

Key features include seamless sharing to Instagram Reels and other platforms, duet and collaboration tools, live streaming capabilities for verified creators, comprehensive analytics dashboard for content performance tracking, and monetization options through brand partnerships and creator funds. The interface prioritizes effortless navigation with swipe-based interactions, engaging comment sections, creator profiles with follower analytics, and advanced privacy controls for content visibility.

Dhuni Music Streaming App

Dhuni - Music Streaming Platform

Crafted a comprehensive music streaming experience that brings millions of songs to users' fingertips. Dhuni offers an immersive audio journey with high-fidelity streaming up to 320kbps, offline download capabilities, and a beautifully designed interface that puts music discovery at the forefront. The platform features curated playlists across 100+ genres, personalized daily mixes based on listening habits, and mood-based radio stations.

The application includes advanced features like collaborative playlists where friends can add songs together, lyrics integration with real-time synchronization, crossfade between tracks for seamless listening, equalizer controls for audio customization, and smart recommendations powered by machine learning algorithms. Users can explore new music through Discovery Weekly, Release Radar, and algorithmically generated playlists tailored to their taste.

Key design elements include an intuitive now-playing screen with gesture controls for quick actions, artist pages with comprehensive discographies and concert information, podcast integration with episode downloads, sleep timer for bedtime listening, and social features allowing users to share their favorite tracks and see what friends are listening to. The interface showcases album artwork prominently with smooth animations, queue management, and seamless integration with car systems and smart speakers through Bluetooth and Chromecast support.
